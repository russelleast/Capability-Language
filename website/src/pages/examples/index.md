# Examples

Examples show DCL as a language for modeling capabilities and their semantics.

## Available Examples

- [Register customer](/Capability-Language/examples/register-customer/)

## Reading Examples

When reading an example, look first for:

- the capability being modeled
- the intent that starts it
- the outcomes it can produce
- the rules and effects that shape those outcomes
- any events, policies, or lifecycle progression
